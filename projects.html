Lévy processes and its applications in derivative pricing

I researched this for my master's thesis and is available to view here.

I initially became interested in Lévy processes and exp-Lévy models as it provided an alternative to the standard Black-Scholes-Merton model, which assumes continuity at any time-scale and no jumps.

In red there is a path of the standard Wiener process (used in BSM model). This is overlaid by EUR/JPY forex data from 17/01/2022 00:00 to 02:00 GMT - which was updated at a 1 second frequency. The discrepancy between the model and actual data is evident, thus the requirement for a new model - the exp-Lévy model.
